Questions

Designed to be clear, fair, and candidate-first.

You stay in control of how your results are used. We never share your assessment without your permission, and you can delete your data at any time.

How long does the assessment take? + −

Most candidates complete it in 8–10 minutes. You can pause and return at any time without losing progress.

Will employers see my full report? + −

No. You control what’s shared. Employers see a tailored summary focused on role alignment and work preferences—not private or sensitive data.

Is there a cost to candidates? + −

The core assessment and your insight report are free for candidates. We partner with employers to keep it that way.
